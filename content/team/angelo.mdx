Angelo is an experienced indie developer, game jammer, and Chickensoft moderator. He's provided tons of help to folks in the community, contributed to the well-received indie game [Arctic Eggs](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2763670/Arctic_Eggs/), and has even finished a few game jams with [something to show for it](https://itch.io/profile/abmarnie).
